\section{Introduction}
The Transformer architecture \cite{vaswani2017attention} replaced recurrent
networks for many sequence-to-sequence tasks. Building on this, BERT
\cite{devlin2019bert} introduced bidirectional masked-language pretraining.

\section{Method}
We follow the standard self-attention formulation \cite{vaswani2017attention}.
Our text encoder is initialised from BERT-base \cite{devlin2019bert}.
